Overview

TreasureShop Phil. uses the traditional management for its record of transactions in a logbook, issuing its ticket, cash counting, and many more. These traditional management systems can lead to clerical and entry errors. To address this, the management system is upgraded into an online-based solution. This makes it hassle-free for both the company and customers.

Objectives

  • Create a hassle-free management system that is easy to access.
  • Enhance management accuracy by reducing errors.
  • Provide easy transaction access for everyone.

Benefits

  • Company: Modernizes operations, making the business attractive and reliable compared to competitors still using manual systems.
  • Customers: Easier access to services, possible online reminders for due dates, and digital receipts.
  • Future Researchers: Offers insights on shifting from manual to automated processes.
  • Staff: Provides an easier and more organized way of handling records.

Core Modules

  • Transaction Management
  • Customer Records
  • Computing Automation
